Gravity Garden

About The Game

Gravity Garden is a beautiful, relaxing physics-based puzzle experience set in lush environments with glowing alien flora and mysterious bioluminescent crystals. Manipulate gravity across 8 distinct levels, managing nutrients to grow a vibrant ecosystem. Play at your own pace in Zen Mode, or test your problem-solving skills in Challenge Mode by completing environmental goals and capturing elusive portals.

Experiment with 4 unique seed types with different physical properties (Standard, Heavy, Bouncy, Light) to discover 14 different plant variations, from glowing mushrooms to towering crystals, each with dynamic growth mechanics.

Features

  • Two Game Modes: Enjoy pressure-free, infinite playtime in Zen Mode, or test your skills in Challenge Mode.
  • Dynamic Gravity: Shift gravity in four directions to navigate puzzles and find unique planting spots on walls and ceilings.
  • Rich Botany: Discover 14 dynamic plant types with unique visual rendering and growth stages.
  • Unique Environments: Explore 8 distinct levels featuring water zones, updrafts, and intricate layouts.
  • Zen Challenges: Complete 20 progressive challenges as you build your tranquil garden.
