Gravity Garden

About The Game

Gravity Garden is a breathtaking, relaxing physics-based puzzle experience set in lush environments with glowing alien flora and mysterious bioluminescent crystals. Utilizing a custom Box2D physics engine, the game features a deep fertility and spawning pipeline where the movement, resting, and impacts of your seed directly influence the growth of your garden. Manipulate gravity in four directions across 8 distinct levels—each boasting unique mechanics like underwater currents, updrafts, and labyrinthine walls—as you manage nutrients to cultivate a vibrant, living ecosystem.

Play at your own pace in the infinite, persistent Zen Mode, or test your problem-solving skills in Challenge Mode by carefully managing a nutrient budget, meeting environmental planting goals, and successfully capturing a wandering black-hole portal. Experiment with 4 unique seed types (Standard, Heavy, Bouncy, Light), each with its own physical properties and locked special plants, to discover 14 different plant variations. Watch in awe as moss, ferns, bioluminescent mushrooms, and towering crystals dynamically grow through multi-pass brushstroke rendering.

The entire experience is brought to life by a stunning custom oil-painting post-processing shader—featuring impasto highlights, canvas weaves, and procedural swirling skies—paired with procedurally generated audio, including wind sounds that dynamically react to your seed's velocity.

Features

  • Two Distinct Game Modes: Enjoy pressure-free, infinite playtime where your garden persists across sessions in Zen Mode, or test your tactical resource management and portal-catching skills in Challenge Mode.
  • Complex Physics & Fertility: Shift gravity on the fly to navigate puzzles. Your seed's speed, impacts, and resting state emit fertility points, dictating where and how lush your flora will spawn.
  • Rich, Dynamic Botany: Discover 14 plant types with continuous growth mechanics. Each plant is beautifully rendered with dark shadow bases, vibrant colors, and bright highlight dabs.
  • Living Environments: Explore 8 handcrafted levels (such as The Cave, The Canyon, and The Tidal Pool) featuring unique environmental forces like water zones and strong updrafts.
  • Procedural Audio & Art: Immerse yourself in a canvas-like world driven by an oil painting shader, procedural swirling skies, "firefly wobble" animations for nutrients, and dynamically generated ambient and wind sounds.
  • 20 Zen Challenges: Complete progressive milestones—from planting your first bloom to mastering gravity manipulation and cultivating massive 50-plant gardens.
