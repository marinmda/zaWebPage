Gravity Warp

About The Game

Gravity Warp is a high-speed, retro pixel-art physics arcade game built natively in Kotlin using the LibGDX framework. Navigate your starship through an endless, toroidal space using inverse-square gravity wells. Touching the screen creates a powerful gravitational pull that you must use to slingshot your ship, collect stardust, and outmaneuver explosive drifting and hunter mines.

Every element in the game—from the 13 unique pixel-art textures to the 4-track ambient synth audio—is generated procedurally at runtime. Experience a dynamic world wrapped in a parallax mesh background that distorts with gravitational lensing when you warp space. And if things get too intense, you can rely on the sophisticated AutoplayAI to take the wheel with its 8-step decision tree and look-ahead simulation.

Features

  • Inverse-Square Gravity Physics: Touch to create gravity wells and navigate your ship using purely physics-driven momentum.
  • Toroidal Screen Wrapping: Objects seamlessly exit one side of the screen and re-enter the other, opening up strategic shortest-path maneuvers.
  • Near-Miss System & Replays: Narrowly escape mines? The game records your close calls and allows you to export them as animated GIFs. Die? Watch an instant 3-second rolling replay.
  • Procedural Art & Audio: All textures and sound effects are synthesized at runtime, keeping the footprint tiny while delivering a rich aesthetic.
  • Built-in Autopilot AI: A complex, spatial-indexed AI algorithm that can take control and pilot the ship to safety.
  • Hard Mode Leaderboards: Double the mine spawn rate and compete for the ultimate high score.
